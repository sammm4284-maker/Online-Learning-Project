About BRIGHT PATH ACADEMY

BRIGHT PATH ACADEMY is a modern education institute dedicated to providing high-quality learning experiences. We focus on skill-based courses, innovative teaching methods and student-centered learning.

Our mission is to empower students with practical knowledge, real-world problem-solving abilities, and strong career-oriented skills to help them succeed in the future.

Admission process

How to Apply

  1. Registration: Fill out the online registration form with your basic details.
  2. Document Submission: Submit your ID proof, academic documents, and recent photograph.
  3. Counselling/Guidance: Our team will guide you in selecting the right course.
  4. Fee Payment: Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.
  5. Enrollment Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation message and course start details.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Minimum Qualification: 10th pass (Some advanced courses require 12th pass).
  2. Age Requirement: Minimum 15 years.
  3. Basic Computer Knowledge: Not mandatory, but helpful.
  4. Documents Required: Aadhar card, passport-size photo, previous mark sheet.
